Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Web Genius ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our students (children) and their parents. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our educational services:

  • Parent Information: Name, email address, and payment information (processed via secure third-party providers).
  • Student Information: First name, age, and projects created within the platform. We do not collect precise geolocation or biometric data.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR)

We strictly adhere to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and GDPR for children:

  • We require verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information from a child under 13.
  • We do not sell student data to third parties.
  • We do not display targeted advertising to students.

4. How We Use Information

We use your data to:

  • Facilitate the online learning course.
  • Track student progress and issue diplomas.
  • Communicate with parents regarding cohort schedules and account updates.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data stored on our servers (via Supabase). However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@becomewebgenius.com.
