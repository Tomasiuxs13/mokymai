Cookie Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website function correctly and remember your preferences.

2. How We Use Cookies

Web Genius uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g., logging into the Student/Parent portal, processing payments).
  • Preference Cookies: Remembering your language setting (EN/LT).
  • Analytics Cookies: We use minimal analytics to see which pages are most popular. We do NOT track individual student browsing habits for advertising.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control specific cookie preferences in your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may prevent you from logging into your account.

4. Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Please check back regularly for any changes.
