Detailed Syllabus

A week-by-week breakdown of our 3-month Live Academy program. Designed to take students from beginners to published creators.

🎨

Month 1: AI Art & Storytelling

Week 1: Generating Magic with Words

Your child will generate their first beautiful piece of AI art and learn exactly how to "talk" to AI safely and effectively using Gemini and Kling AI.

Week 2: Designing Custom Characters

They'll invent a unique hero or villain and learn the secret to keeping their character looking exactly the same across different scenes and styles.

Week 3: Building Entire Worlds

From cyberpunk cities to magical forests, students will generate stunning backdrops and environments to set the stage for their story.

🏆

Portfolio Checkpoint: The AI Graphic Novel

Deliverable: Kids will combine their characters, worlds, and AI-generated dialogue into a polished, printable 5-page digital comic book.

🎬

Month 2: Video, Audio & Animation

Week 5: Giving Characters a Voice

Students will use AI to generate realistic voiceovers, clone custom voices safely, and compose original background music to set the mood.

Week 6: Bringing Images to Life

The magic moment: transforming static pictures into cinematic motion clips and making characters actually speak with lip-sync technology.

Week 7: Directing the Edit

Learning pro-level video editing basics in CapCut to assemble their generated clips, add impactful transitions, and perfectly sync the audio.

🎬

Portfolio Checkpoint: Animated Movie Trailer

Deliverable: Your child will direct and premiere a breathtaking 60-second video trailer for their original story concept.

🚀

Month 3: AI Apps & Automation

Week 9: Building Apps Without Code

Students will use AI agents to build a fully functional web app or game interface in hours, not weeks—no complex coding required.

Week 10: The Magic of Automation

Learning how to make different AI tools talk to each other automatically using n8n, saving time and creating powerful workflows.

Week 11: Connecting the Dots

Integrating the art, voices, and videos they created in Months 1 & 2 into their web app to create a rich, interactive multimedia experience.

🏆

Portfolio Checkpoint: AI Powered App

Deliverable: Students will publish a live web app or interactive game and present their entire portfolio to parents and peers on Demo Day.
