Detailed Syllabus

A week-by-week breakdown of our 3-month Live Academy program. Designed to take students from beginners to published creators.

Month 1: AI Art & Storytelling

Week 1: Introduction to Generative AI

Understanding how LLMs and Image Generators work. Safety, ethics, and "prompt engineering" basics using Gemini and Kling AI.

Week 2: Character Design & Consistency

Creating consistent characters for stories. Learning seed numbers, style references, and remixing.

Week 3: World Building & Environments

Generating landscapes, interiors, and consistent settings for your game or movie concepts.

Week 4: Project 1 — The Graphic Novel

Deliverable: Create a 5-page digital comic book using AI-generated art and story assistance.

Month 2: Video, Audio & Animation

Week 5: AI Voice & Sound Design

cloning voices (ethically), generating sound effects, and composing background music with AI tools.

Week 6: Image to Video Animation

Bringing static characters to life. Lip-syncing and basic motion animation.

Week 7: Video Editing Fundamentals

Using CapCut / Premiere to assemble clips, add transitions, and sync audio.

Week 8: Project 2 — The Movie Trailer

Deliverable: Produce a 60-second trailer for your story concept using animated AI assets.

Month 3: AI Apps & Automation

Week 9: AI Agents & No-Code Building

Introduction to Nano Banana and Antigravity. Building complex apps without writing code from scratch.

Week 10: Automation with n8n

Connecting AI tools together. Creating automated workflows to process data and content.

Week 11: Advanced App Logic

Integrating ElevenLabs and Suno into your app logic for a rich multimedia experience.

Week 12: Final Demo Day

Deliverable: Present your final portfolio website and live project to parents and peers.
